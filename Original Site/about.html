About Our Company

Providing Complete Generator System Sales and Service for Residential, Commercial and Industrial Clients, and Compressor/CNG Service and Repair.

For over a decade, Varitech, Inc. has been providing exceptional sales and service of generator systems and CNG equipment to residential and commercial businesses with a dedication to excellence in all we do. Our generator division offers complete Generator Systems for Residential, Commercial and Industrial clients. We’ll help you determine the size generator you need, then provide complete installation, as well as preventative maintenance, parts, service and repair to keep your generator system ready for the next power outage. Already have a generator system? No problem! We provide service for all sizes, makes and models.

Our Compressor/CNG division provides parts, service, repair and preventative maintenance for air compressors and compressed natural gas fueling stations. The common thread is our commitment to service and a win-win mindset. Give us a try and see for yourself why Varitech is your best choice for all your CNG and Compressor needs.

Contact our professional team of experts for all your generator system and compressed natural gas system needs at (502) 966-3400 or by email at info@vteng.com.
